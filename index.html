Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains clearly and honestly how መዝገበ ስብሐት — an educational application rooted in the sacred teachings of the Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church — handles information on your device.
§ 1

Information Collection & Use

The App itself does not collect or store personal data such as your name, email address, or phone number. However, the App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you and your device for advertising and analytics purposes.

§ 2

Access to Media Files

The App does not access your photos or videos without your consent. When you use features that require an image — such as reporting a bug — the App uses the system picker exclusively.

  • The App accesses the selected file only temporarily for that specific action.
  • The App does not request broad access to your entire media library.
  • Images selected for bug reports are transmitted to our support team to help resolve technical issues.
§ 3

App Content & Local Storage

All audio and text content provided in the App is for educational and devotional purposes.

  • Audio files may be cached locally on your device to allow offline access.
  • App preferences and theme settings are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to us.
