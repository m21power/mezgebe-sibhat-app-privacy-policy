# Privacy Policy

**መዝገበ ስብሐት** (“the App”) is an educational application designed to provide access to the sacred teachings of the Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how the App handles user information.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
The App itself **does not collect or store personal data** such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers. However, the App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you and your device for advertising and analytics purposes.

## 2. Access to Media Files
The App **does not access your photos or videos without your consent**. When you use features that require an image (such as reporting a bug), the App uses the **system picker**. 

- The App accesses the selected file **only temporarily** for that specific action.  
- The App does **not request broad access** to your entire media library.  
- Images selected for bug reports are transmitted to our support team to help resolve technical issues.

## 3. App Content and Local Storage
All audio and text content provided in the App is for educational purposes.

- Audio files may be **cached locally on the user’s device** to allow offline access.  
- App preferences and theme settings are stored **locally on the device only**.  

## 4. Third-Party Services & Advertising
The App uses third-party services that may collect data to provide advertisements and analyze app performance. These services include:

* **Google AdMob:** Used to display advertisements. Google may use the **Advertising ID (AAID)** from your device to personalize ads and measure ad performance. You can manage your ad preferences in your device settings.
* **Google Play Services:** Used for app functionality and maintenance.

These third-party providers have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them:
- [Google Play Services Privacy Policy](https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/)
- [AdMob Privacy Policy](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543?hl=en)

## 5. Data Security
We value your trust. While the App does not collect personal identifiers, we use industry-standard SDKs that implement security measures to protect the data processed for advertising and app stability.

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
